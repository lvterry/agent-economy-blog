Google introduces Gemma 4 — the most intelligent open models to date. Purpose-built for advanced reasoning and agentic workflows, Gemma 4 delivers an unprecedented level of intelligence-per-parameter.

## Model Sizes

Gemma 4 comes in four versatile sizes, moving beyond simple chat to handle complex logic and agentic workflows:

- **E2B (Effective 2B)** — Edge-optimized, 128K context window
- **E4B (Effective 4B)** — Edge-optimized with native audio input support
- **26B MoE** — Mixture of Experts, activates only 3.8B parameters, latency-focused
- **31B Dense** — Dense model maximizing raw quality, 256K context window

## Core Capabilities

- **Advanced Reasoning** — Multi-step planning and deep logic, significant improvements in math and instruction-following
- **Agentic Workflows** — Native support for function-calling, structured JSON output, system instructions
- **Code Generation** — High-quality offline code, turning workstations into local AI coding assistants
- **Multimodal** — Native video and image processing, OCR and chart understanding; E2B/E4B support audio input
- **Long Context** — 128K for edge models, 256K for larger models, can process entire codebases
- **140+ Languages** — Natively trained on over 140 languages

## Performance

The 31B model ranks #3 among open models on Arena AI text leaderboard, with the 26B model at #6. Gemma 4 outcompetes models 20x its size.

## Open Source License

Gemma 4 is released under Apache 2.0 license, providing complete developer flexibility and digital sovereignty for building and deploying across any environment.
