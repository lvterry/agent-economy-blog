tinygrad is an extremely simple yet powerful neural network framework. It breaks down the most complex neural networks into three **OpTypes**: ElementwiseOps, ReduceOps, and MovementOps.

## Why tinygrad?

Unlike traditional frameworks, tinygrad follows the principle of minimalism:

- **ElementwiseOps**: Unary, binary, ternary ops like SQRT, LOG2, ADD, MUL
- **ReduceOps**: Reduction ops like SUM, MAX
- **MovementOps**: Virtual ops like RESHAPE, PERMUTE, EXPAND
