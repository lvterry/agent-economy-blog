OpenAI announces it will shut down the Sora app, just months after launching the AI video generation tool.

## What This Means

For users: Content created with Sora won't be lost. Timeline for app and API shutdown will be shared soon.

For the industry: This is a major adjustment in OpenAI's video generation strategy, possibly due to increased competition or shifting priorities.
