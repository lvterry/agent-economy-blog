Project NOMAD is a free, open-source offline server that lets anyone run a complete AI system on their own computer — no internet required.

## What This Means

For regular users: Access Wikipedia, medical references, AI assistants, and offline maps without internet. Great for emergency preparedness, off-grid living, or remote areas.

For tech users: Run Ollama LLMs locally, self-host your knowledge base, and own your data. Hardware requirements are reasonable (32GB RAM + decent integrated graphics or entry-level GPU).
