Arm released its first self-developed data center chip — Arm AGI CPU — designed specifically for agentic AI infrastructure.

## What This Means

For the industry: In the agentic AI era, the CPU becomes the "orchestrator" of modern infrastructure — managing thousands of distributed tasks, coordinating accelerators, scheduling workloads. Arm AGI CPU delivers 2x+ performance per rack vs latest x86 systems.

For developers: Arm's influence in AI data centers continues to grow. Meta is the lead partner; Cerebras, Cloudflare, OpenAI and others are early customers.
