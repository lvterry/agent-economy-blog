Variant is an AI-powered design tool where you simply enter an idea for an app or website and browse endless design options by scrolling.

## Core Experience

Unlike traditional design tools, Variant uses a "scroll to explore" interaction:

- Enter your idea or description
- AI generates multiple design options in real-time
- Browse different variations like scrolling through short videos
- Quickly find design directions that match your vision

## Use Cases

Perfect for quickly getting design inspiration: landing pages, product interfaces, marketing sites, etc. Serves as a starting point for the design process, helping teams explore more possibilities in early stages.
