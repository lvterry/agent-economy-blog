Anthropic today announced a $65 billion Series H funding round led by Altimeter Capital, Dragoneer, Greenoaks, and Sequoia Capital, valuing the company at $965 billion post-money. It is one of the largest single funding rounds in AI history.

## Explosive Growth

Anthropic disclosed that its annualized revenue crossed $47 billion earlier this month, up dramatically since its Series G in February. Global enterprises across industries are deploying Claude in core operations, and a growing number of individual users rely on it for everyday work.

## Massive Compute Infrastructure Buildout

A primary use of the new capital is compute expansion. Anthropic recently signed major infrastructure deals: up to 5 gigawatts of new capacity with Amazon, 5 gigawatts of next-generation TPU capacity with Google and Broadcom, and GPU access at Colossus 1 and 2 through SpaceX. Claude is the first frontier model available on all three major cloud platforms — AWS, Google Cloud, and Microsoft Azure.

The round also brings in strategic infrastructure partners Micron, Samsung, and SK hynix — memory, storage, and logic chip leaders critical to scaling compute reliably.

## Stellar Investor Lineup

Beyond the lead investors, the round includes Capital Group, Coatue, D1 Capital Partners, GIC, ICONIQ, XN as co-leads. Blackstone, Brookfield, Fidelity, General Catalyst, Insight Partners, Jane Street, Lightspeed, Temasek, and many others participated. The round also incorporates $15 billion in previously committed hyperscaler investments including $5 billion from Amazon.

## A Watershed Moment for AI Commercialization

This funding round marks a pivotal moment for the AI industry's transition from research exploration to full-scale commercialization. Anthropic has evolved from a company known primarily for AI safety research into an enterprise giant with $47 billion in annualized revenue approaching a trillion-dollar valuation. The demand for AI infrastructure remains insatiable as compute requirements for both training and inference continue growing exponentially.
